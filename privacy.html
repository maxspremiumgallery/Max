Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 13, 2026

1. Introduction

Max's Premium Gallery ("we", "us", or "our") operates a subscription-based content channel on Telegram. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you interact with our Telegram bot or channels.

By subscribing or interacting with our service, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

When you interact with our Telegram bot or channels, we may collect:

  • Your Telegram user ID
  • Your Telegram username (if public)
  • Your first and last name as set in Telegram
  • Subscription plan, payment method, and subscription dates
  • Payment proof screenshots (for manual verification)
  • Messages sent to our bot for support purposes

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Manage your subscription and grant access to premium content
  • Process and verify your payments
  • Send important notifications about your subscription
  • Provide customer support
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Improve our services

4. Payment Information

We do not store credit card or bank account details. Payments are processed through:

  • Telegram Stars — processed natively and securely by Telegram
  • Cryptocurrency — processed via third-party wallets
  • Buy Me a Coffee (BMC) — processed by the BMC platform

For cryptocurrency and BMC payments, your transaction may be visible on public blockchains or the BMC platform. We only record proof screenshots and transaction references you share with us.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • With payment processors as necessary to complete transactions

6. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in a secure, encrypted database on our private server. We restrict access to authorized administrators only. Sensitive credentials are encrypted at rest using industry-standard AES-256 encryption.

However, no internet-based system can be guaranteed 100% secure. We encourage you to keep your Telegram account secure.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as you have an active subscription plus 12 months thereafter. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.
